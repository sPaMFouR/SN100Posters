One hundred years of supernova science

In 1925, Knut Lundmark identified a class of exceptionally bright novae—later named "supernovae" by Baade and Zwicky, marking the beginning of supernova research. To commemorate 100 years of this discovery, we invite the global supernova community to a centennial conference in Stockholm, August 18–22, 2025. Add the link to upload the posters

One hundred years of supernova science

All e-Poster presenters are invited to give a flash talk during their allotted session. This short pitch should highlight the main idea of your e-poster and should be presented using a single static slide in PDF format (landscape, 16:9). Please avoid animations or videos. The goal is to briefly introduce your work and encourage attendees to visit your poster online.

We kindly ask you to upload both your flash-talk slide and e-Poster as PDF files by August 11, at the following link : Supernova 100 - Poster Storage. For the filenames, please use the format 'ePoster_Title_Surname.pdf' and 'FlashTalk_Title_Surname.pdf'. Posters will be hosted online and will go live on August 16 22:00 UTC. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at supernova100y@gmail.com.
